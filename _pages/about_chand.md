### Saroj Chand

Saroj Chand is a postdoctoral scholar in Physics at the University of California, Berkeley and an incoming Assistant Professor and Simons Foundation Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the University at Buffalo, starting in Spring 2027.

His research focuses on quantum sensing, precision metrology, solid-state spin defects, and quantum materials. He develops experimental platforms based on nitrogen-vacancy centers in diamond, optical microscopy, microwave control, and nanoscale measurement techniques to probe magnetic, electronic, and material phenomena.

The Chand Precision Quantum Lab will develop scalable quantum sensing and precision measurement platforms for applications in condensed matter physics, quantum materials, quantum information science, and nanoscale imaging.

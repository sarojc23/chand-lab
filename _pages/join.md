We are a **brand-new group** and recruiting at every level. Building experiments
from the ground up means early members shape the lab's direction and get deep,
hands-on experience across optics, cryogenics, electronics, and quantum materials.

## PhD students

We are looking for students excited about quantum sensing, atomic-scale defects,
and 2D materials. A background in physics, electrical engineering, applied physics,
or materials science is a good fit. Hands-on experience with optics, cryogenics,
RF/microwave electronics, or scientific programming (Python) is a plus but not
required — curiosity and persistence matter more.

**Prospective students:** apply to the
[UB ECE PhD program](https://engineering.buffalo.edu/electrical.html) and mention
your interest in the Chand Lab. Feel free to email first.

## Postdocs

We expect to hire postdocs as funding develops. If you have experience in
NV/quantum sensing, cryogenic optical measurement, or 2D quantum materials,
reach out with your CV and a short statement of interests.

## Undergraduates

UB undergraduates interested in research are encouraged to email. Projects can
range from optics and instrumentation to data analysis and simulation.

## How to reach us

Email **sarojc@buffalo.edu** with:
- a short note on what excites you about the lab,
- your CV, and
- (for PhD/postdoc) a sentence or two on relevant experience.
